Lesson 3: Places

Lección 3: Lugares

At School (En el colegio)

Classroom

Salón de clases

The place where we study and learn with teachers.

Library

Biblioteca

A quiet place to read books and do homework.

Playground

Patio de juegos

The area where we play and have fun during recess.

Gym

Gimnasio

Where we practice sports and exercise.

At Home (En la casa)

Living Room

Sala de estar

Where the family watches TV and relaxes together.

Kitchen

Cocina

The place where we cook and prepare delicious food.

Bedroom

Dormitorio

Your private room where you sleep and rest.

Garden

Jardín

The outdoor area with plants, grass, and flowers.

Bathroom

Baño

The place for personal hygiene and taking a shower.

Sentence of the day:

"I am in the garden"

Yo estoy en el jardín
